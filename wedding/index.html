How we met.

June 13, 2014

Although we spent an entire year in high school without knowing each other, we only met the next summer on a band trip to Hawaii. Students were required to go out in groups of 5 or more, and Spencer tagged along with my group. We ended up having so much fun and spent the rest of the trip together. Our favorite memory was the luau and riding back on the bus together.


The proposal.

December 16, 2020

Spencer left early from work to pick up the ring after it arrived at the store. When I got home from my hair appointment and noticed Spencer was not there, I texted to ask where he was. He replied, "I'm getting gas." When Spencer got home, he asked if I wanted to go out for dinner and look at Christmas lights. We drove around town looking at lights and made a stop near a pond to get a closer look. It was here where Spencer got down on one knee and asked if I would marry him!
